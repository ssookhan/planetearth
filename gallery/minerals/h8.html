Columbite Group - (Fe2+,Mn2+,Mg)(Nb,Ta)2O6

Oxides

Habit: Crystals are brownish-black, short prismatic, equant, also flat tabular black, massive. Opaque, translucent through thin edges, subMetallic Luster


Environment: An accessory mineral of granitic Pegmatites and other environments where Nb > Ta. Rarely found in alkalic Pegmatites and an alteration mineral of other Nb-rich minerals.


Etymology: Named for Columbia, source of the first specimens.


An important ore of tantalum and niobium.
