Legrandite - Zn2(AsO4)(OH)·H2O

Arsenates

Habit: Bright yellow, wax yellow or colorless. Prismatic crystals, typically in sprays. Vitreous luster; transparent to translucent. White streak.


Environment: A secondary mineral in the oxidized zones of zinc/arsenic-bearing deposits.


Etymology: Named after M. Legrand, a Belgian mine manager who collected the first specimen.
