Sandstones
These well-layered strata are sandstones. They have no fossils or dateable material.
Xenolith
Xenoliths are country rocks that have been included into an intrusion.
Basalt
This blackish-green rock-type is basalt. These basalts contain no dateable material.
Limestones
The blocky bluish-grey rocks are limestones. They contain an ash layer that can be dated to 200 million years old.
Shales
The streaky greyish strata are shales. They contain fossils that contrain the age to the Paleozoic.
